Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

Overview

MacPadPlusPlus is a local macOS text and log editor. The app is designed to process your files on-device.

Data Collection

MacPadPlusPlus does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

File Handling

Files you open are read from locations you choose. The app may store local preferences and recent-file metadata on your Mac for usability.

Third-Party Services

MacPadPlusPlus does not require a cloud account and does not upload your document contents to remote servers.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/madmunky/MacPadPlusPlus-public/issues.

Changes

This policy may be updated with new app releases. Updates will be posted on this page.
