FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common setup, performance, and troubleshooting questions.

What files can TextPad++ open?

TextPad++ is designed for plain text and log files, including very large logs.

How do I improve performance on huge files?

Open files directly from local storage, avoid heavy background tasks, and allow indexing to finish before searching repeatedly.

Where are recent files and preferences stored?

The app stores support files in your user Application Support directory under the app name.

Does TextPad++ send my files to a server?

No. Editing and search operations are local to your Mac.

How do I report a crash or bug?

Open a GitHub issue with your app version, macOS version, and exact repro steps.
